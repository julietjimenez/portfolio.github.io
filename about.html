The Backstory

Hello, I'm Juliet

A UX Designer from Chicago and in my final year at Purdue University. I create needed, wanted and desired experiences that are driven by user research insights. I'm graduating in December 2020 with a Bachelors of Science in UX Design and two minors in Communication and Psychology. Through my classes at Purdue, I have worked with small and large sponsors alike - Crema, Military Family Research Institute, Dionysus and Stryker. Outside of school, I worked as the Graphics Editor for the Purdue Exponent and as an Undergraduate Research Assistant on two faculty projects. I was also a UI Design Intern with YEP Nation and am currently with the Military Family Research Institute improving the ux of their digital platforms.

I'm on the hunt for an inspiring full-time ux or product design role following my graduation in December 2020!
