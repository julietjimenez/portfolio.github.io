Stryker + UE Group

Overview

Stryker is a medical technology company that creates products and services for a variety of medical industries. They teamed up with UE Group, a UX consultancy, and sponsored a class project during the Fall of 2019 where myself and four other UX students were asked to create a mobile app for providing surgery updates to those waiting for patients.

Project Type

Sponsored Class Project

Sponsor

Stryker, UE Group

Final Deliverables

Mobile app concept and a user research report

Team

Madison Lindeman, Simmi Bansal, Eleanor Hamilton, Rheaa Kamath, Juliet Jimenez

Length

15 Weeks (Aug 2018 - Dec 2018)

Low to Medium Fidelity Designs

I had a heavy hand in our low and high fidelity designs but not medium fidelity for this project. Therefore, I have shown below my low fidelity designs that were influential to how we prototyped out the medium fidelity. It's important to note that the last two medium fidelity designs are not mine. However, the first was done by me.

High Fidelity Prototype

This is the final prototype I created based off our low and medium fidelity designs, sponsor feedback, and class feedback. It's interactive so feel free to click around! The app is able to give those waiting remotely or in the waiting room information about each stage of surgery and when it's completed to ease their anxiety. Moreover, it can provide users with updates about the surgery status every 30 minutes or hour (depending on settings).
