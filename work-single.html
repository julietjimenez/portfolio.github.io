Star Behavioral Health Providers (SBHP)

Role Information Hierarchy, Interface Design
Team Meena Nanduri, Jessie Zhou, Megan Ledford, Kyle Milne, Juliet Jimenez
Company Military Family Research Institute at Purdue University

The Context: This project was completed in Experience Studio - a UX Design class we take every semester to apply the concepts we learn to real projects with real sponsors. My team and I had the opportunity to work with the Military Family Research Institute at Purdue (MFRI) - an organization that works to help better the lives of military families through community outreach and research. A year later, I now work as a Student UX Designer for MFRI helping maintain this website and other resources.

The Project: We specifically worked with Star Behavioral Health Providers, or SBHP, a suborganization within MFRI that provides specialized trainings to mental health care providers so they can better treat military veterans and their families. In turn, these providers are listed on the SBHP website as a resource for military families during their search for a mental health provider. This is where our team came in - MFRI asked us to complete a total redesign of their website both structurally and visually. This project became a year long one due to how large the website was (and is) with two different sides, one for providers and one for services. For clarity's sake, our team worked on the services side.
